Welcome

Welcome to jgwalsh.net. This site highlights my background in psychology, behavioral expertise, and law enforcement readiness. My journey began with clinical experience at McLean Hospital and the McLean-Franciscan partnership, where I honed my skills in understanding human behavior and mental health.

With a foundation in academic training and hands-on clinical work, I transitioned into the field of law enforcement readiness. This shift was driven by a passion to integrate psychological principles into critical decision-making and operational strategies.
