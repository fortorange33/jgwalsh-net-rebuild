Selected Projects

The projects below represent my applied work in behavioral analysis, law enforcement data visualization, and analytical problem-solving. Each integrates elements of psychological insight, statistical modeling, and operational relevance.

Law Enforcement Transition Model

Developed a structured framework outlining career transition from clinical psychology to investigative roles within federal and state law enforcement. Grounded in operational psychology and analytic reasoning.

2022

Built an SPSS-based framework to structure, score, and visualize behavioral risk indicators drawn from clinical settings. Adaptable for pre-screening, monitoring, or post-incident review.

Tools: SPSS, case coding, descriptive stats, visual analytics
